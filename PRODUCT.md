# Product

## Register
product

## Product Purpose
A private medical prescription tracker for tracking cannabis prescriptions across multiple family members. The user manages prescription validity, pack usage, refill status, and license renewal reminders — primarily from their phone before or during pharmacy visits.

## Users
A primary caregiver or family head in Israel managing cannabis medical prescriptions for up to 4 tracked people. Non-technical. Uses the app briefly and frequently. Primary device: mobile phone.

## Core Tasks (in priority order)
1. See at a glance who has expiring prescriptions and how many packs remain
2. Record pack usage when a prescription is dispensed
3. Know when to renew the medical license (last prescription alert)
4. Upload a new prescription PDF and confirm detected dates and pack count
5. Review notification history

## Tone
Trustworthy. Calm. Organized. Practical. Feels like a well-maintained personal health record, not a SaaS dashboard.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS: purple/blue gradients, hero metrics, "growth" language
- Clinical/hospital: sterile white + teal, dense data tables, institutional fonts
- Alarming: red banners everywhere, aggressive warnings for routine states
- Playful: rounded everything, emoji-heavy, consumer health app pastels

## Strategic Principles
- Information hierarchy over decoration. The most urgent prescription must be visible in under 3 seconds.
- Calm urgency: expiring soon is important, not alarming.
- The interface should disappear into the task. No chrome for its own sake.
- Mobile-first. Most interactions happen in one hand, standing in a pharmacy.
